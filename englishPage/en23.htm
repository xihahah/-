Jiangsu education policy introduction

June 7th, 2018 01:50 Sina watch point author teacher certificate review resources

Policy benefits and guarantees

Graduates of the "three supports and one assistance" scheme enjoy the following benefits:

(1) during the service period, the college graduates of "three supports and one assistance" shall, with reference to the standard of salary income level of the newly employed staff employed by the local township public institutions after the probation period for the new graduates, determine the standard of working and living allowances, which shall be paid monthly. The welfare enjoys the same treatment as the employees of the service unit. From the month of starting salary, attend worker of local enterprise each society is sure, pay housing accumulation fund, plan hair housing allowance, take its work to live allowance standard as pay cardinal number. Individual pay is partly by the unit that issues working life allowance is in charge of in individual allowance withhold acting capture. For the service period of "three support and one help" college graduates to handle personal accident insurance. Relevant expenses shall be included into the scope of work and living subsidies granted by the finance department. In order to guarantee the implementation of various benefits, during the service period, the provincial government will allocate the central government subsidy funds according to the standard of 22,000 yuan per person per year, and the insufficient part and the recruitment plan beyond the provincial level will independently recruit "three supports and one assistance" college graduates, which will be borne by the local county (city, district) finance. In addition, according to the standard of 2,000 yuan per person, the central government will grant a one-time settlement allowance to each newly recruited "three branches and one assistance" personnel who have served on duty for more than six months.

(2) every year, a certain number of civil service positions will be offered, and graduates of basic service projects such as "three support and one assistance" will be specially recruited after the service has expired.

(3) every year, a certain number of positions in public institutions shall be allocated, and graduates of basic service projects such as "three supports and one assistance", which have passed the evaluation of the targeted recruitment service.

(4) each year, a certain number of posts in provincial or city-owned state-owned enterprises will be assigned, and graduates from "three support and one support" universities will be specially recruited and qualified after the expiry of the service evaluation.

(5) if the graduates of "three supports and one assistance" who have passed the examination after the service period have applied for the master's degree in provincial universities within three years, the total score of the preliminary examination will be increased by 10 points, and they will be admitted first under the same conditions. For graduates who have been admitted as postgraduates to participate in the "three supports and one support" program, the university should retain their admission qualifications during the service period. After the expiry of the service period, those who have passed the examination will be exempted from admission to education junior college.

(6) if the "three supports and one assistance" college graduates start their own businesses after the service period expires, it shall be incorporated into the policy support range for college graduates to start their own businesses, and provide them with "one-stop" services such as policy consultation, project development, entrepreneurship training, business incubation, small loan, opening guidance and follow-up guidance. In accordance with relevant policies, administrative fees can be waived for those who are qualified to engage in individual business; To pass all sorts of form flexible obtain employment, what accord with a regulation can enjoy social insurance allowance.

(7) if, after the expiration of the service period, the graduates of "three supports and one assistance" choose their own careers, the human resources and social security departments at all levels and their public employment and talent service institutions shall provide free policy consultation, career guidance and career introduction services; To organize and participate in vocational qualification training, vocational skill appraisal or employment internship, and provide vocational training subsidies according to regulations; After the service expires, the unemployed for a long time "three support and one assistance" college graduates to carry out the key assistance.
