Shandong's new rule on targeted skills for poverty alleviation: eligible students can enjoy the "five exemptions and one benefit" policy

2018-01-09 11:18:00　Source: new public　Author: Zhang Chunxiao

On January 8, reporter learned from shandong people club hall that will further promote the implementation of the entire province the mechanic colleges and universities in our province service poverty engines work plan of action, by 2020, each will have attended the mechanic colleges by inputting tent card poor families should be at the beginning of the previous workers and retired soldiers, graduated from high school and failed to continue studies can be free to accept the mechanic education, each have a ability to work and to participate in vocational training will cross tent card poor laborer to the mechanic colleges and universities accept every year at least one free vocational training, to help poor families in mechanic education or vocational training students (trainees) employment desire, to achieve "education training, Get rid of poverty and become rich.

Recently, the province people club hall, poverty relief office jointly issued by the on the mechanic colleges and universities in shandong province service action plan for poverty alleviation engines work, relying on the province the mechanic colleges and universities, to develop the mechanic education, vocational training, assistance for poverty alleviation of poverty alleviation, love the educational poverty alleviation work, workers' entrepreneurship skills for new jobs, a sharp rise in the poor areas in a more accurate measure, the extraordinary strength, promote the further development of poverty alleviation engines work.

According to the introduction, the personnel who enjoy the targeted skills poverty alleviation policy must be the personnel who failed to continue their study and retired soldiers after graduating from high school within the scope of the establishment file card of the local poverty alleviation department. The students who meet the requirements shall be subject to the "five exemptions and one entitlement" policy, that is, tuition fee exemption, accommodation fee exemption, materials fee exemption, free work-study position provision, free recommendation of employment position, and national financial aid policy. For rural poor families whose children receive stitching CARDS from skilled workers education, the subsidy will be provided according to the standard of 3,000 yuan per year per student. According to the notice, the policy of vocational training subsidy should be implemented for the poor people of the right age who receive the precise training of vocational skills. The poor students who participate in the professional vocational skills appraisal when they graduate from the technical colleges and universities are exempt from the professional skills appraisal fee and the certificate fee.

The circular requires that green channels be opened to provide the highest quality professional and highest quality employment. The human resources and social security departments of all cities shall organize the technical colleges within their jurisdiction (including the central and provincial technical schools) to actively apply for poverty alleviation work of skills, and identify the technical colleges with perfect school conditions, high quality of teaching, wide range of employment, high employment rate and high quality of employment as those for targeted poverty alleviation work. Technical colleges undertaking targeted skills poverty alleviation work should include majors with broad enrollment and employment, abundant teaching faculty, excellent practical training equipment, high social recognition and good employment situation into targeted skills poverty alleviation programs. The human resources and social security bureau of each city shall release the colleges and majors of poverty alleviation skilled workers and professionals to the society before the end of February each year and report them to the provincial human resources and social security department for record, so that students from poor families and retired soldiers can choose to study. To undertake the dynamic adjustment of colleges and majors.

For technical colleges and universities that have achieved remarkable results in carrying out targeted skills poverty alleviation work, they will give priority to support under the same conditions in the implementation of projects related to the national plan for the revitalization of highly skilled personnel, the selection of featured famous schools for provincial skilled workers, the selection of featured workstations for qilu skill masters and the pilot of new apprenticeship system for enterprises.
