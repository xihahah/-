What is the policy of supporting education, how does the country give subsidy, go supporting education qualification?

1. The policy

(1) the former service unit should give priority to receiving "three support and one support" college students who have passed the assessment after the service period. County, township, all kinds of institutions, when there are vacancies to supplement personnel, should also be out a certain position to absorb this part of graduates. Those who start their own business after the expiration of the service period may enjoy the relevant policies such as administrative fee reduction, small loan guarantee and discount interest.

(2) to serve for more than 2 years in western regions and arduous and remote regions. For those who apply for master degree in 3 years after the service period, the total score of the preliminary examination is increased by 10 points, and they will be admitted first under the same conditions.

(3) personnel departments at all levels shall establish a special talent pool for "three supports and one assistance" college students, extensively collect information about the job requirements of various employers, mobilize all kinds of employers to accept "three supports and one assistance" college students, and provide employment guidance and recommendation to help them find employment units.

(4) for those who enter state-owned enterprises and institutions, the salary standards for their positions shall be determined by the receiving units according to the comparison of their positions with those under the same conditions; The service term shall be calculated as working years. In the future promotion of high support, under the same conditions of priority.

2. Conditions

Recruitment target is the province and the province's source of students outside the province's general college graduates, without orientation, appointment and employment units have been implemented graduates. The basic conditions are:

(1) good political quality, love the socialist motherland, and support the party's basic line and policies;

(2) college degree or above with professional knowledge required for a job;

(3) be dedicated, law-abiding and upright;

(4) good health.

3. Recruitment principles and procedures

Recruitment insists on the principle of "openness, equality, competition and merit" principle, professional in rural grassroots in urgent need of agriculture, forestry, water conservancy, medicine, education, economy class, priority of family economic difficulties students who are recruited at the same time, priority arrangement is highly educated graduates, priority arrangement has been earning a graduate student graduates, give priority to arrange material graduates retrogradation. Recruitment will be conducted according to the following procedures:：

(1) make plans

Every year on April 20, cities "three helps" office to collect, summarize the local villages and towns level of education, agriculture, health, poverty alleviation, such as basic jobs demand information, and report to the province "three help" the office, the office according to unified planning and relevant requirements, comprehensive municipal recruiting position and quantity is determined, submit a leadership team, the formation of the province's annual "three helps" recruiting plan, released in May each year for society.

(2) organization and recruitment

The office of work coordination and management at all levels and universities should make full use of the media such as radio, television, newspapers and periodicals, and the Internet to publicize relevant policies and actively mobilize college graduates to sign up.

(3) confirm the candidate

In late June, the provincial office of "three support and one help" reviewed the selected candidates in each city, and designated the time and hospital to carry out physical examination on the selected students.

(4) training on duty

"Three support and one help" college students before work, to focus on training. Training contents about grass-roots work, especially for the party and the state's policies on the aspects such as agriculture, rural areas and farmers, the present situation of the grass-roots work, the region is proposed to service units and position, etc., the basic situation of the service at the same time should also introduces the life, people, customs and so on to, to help them adapt to the working and living environment more quickly.

According to the unified deployment of the provincial "three support and one assistance" office, the training shall be organized by the provincial education department, agricultural department, health department, poverty relief office and other departments according to different service project personnel. The training period is usually 5 days.

The recruited college graduates will be issued a recruitment notice by the provincial office of "three support and one support". After receiving the recruitment notice, the graduates shall report to the personnel department of the service county at the specified time and place. For those who fail to report for the specified time, the recruitment will be cancelled.

College graduates engaged in "three support and one support" work, may be included in the school year employment statistics. (1) living and transportation subsidies

During the service period, "three supports and one assistance" college students can enjoy certain living and transportation subsidies, with the subsidy standard of 800 yuan per person per month. Each county (city, district) "three support and one help" office for the graduates press the provincial "three support and one help" office designated agricultural bank card and responsible for the monthly distribution.

(2) insurance

Service county "three support and one support" office according to the province "three support and one support" office uniform requirements for "three support and one support" college students to handle personal accident insurance and hospitalized medical insurance.

(3) medical expenses

The standard of medical examination for college students is 300 yuan per person.

(4) sources of funds

The funds required for the "three support and one assistance" plan shall be solved by special financial arrangement funds. Of these, the living allowance, transportation subsidy and insurance expense for 30 economically deficient counties shall be borne by the provincial, municipal, county (city, district) finance in a ratio of 5:3:2. The living, transportation subsidies and insurance expenses for services to other counties (cities and districts) shall be borne by the financial burden of the host cities and counties (cities and districts). The cost of physical examination and training for "three supports and one assistance" college students shall be borne by the provincial finance department, and the work expenses shall be borne by the finance department at all levels.

"Three supports and one assistance" refers to the work of supporting agriculture, supporting education, supporting medicine and alleviating poverty in rural areas. The policy of the plan is based on document no. 16 issued by the ministry of personnel of the People's Republic of China, notice on organizing college graduates to work in supporting education, supporting agriculture, supporting medicine and supporting poverty at rural grassroots level. The purpose of the plan is to provide specific guidance and guarantee for college graduates to implement employment problems at grassroots units. [details of the three-support and one-help plan]

1. Plan implementation method: open recruitment, voluntary registration, organization selection, unified dispatch, from 2006 for five consecutive years, about 20,000 college graduates every year, mainly arranged to work in villages and towns for supporting education, supporting agriculture, supporting medicine and poverty alleviation.

2. Implementation process: it mainly includes two aspects of organizational recruitment and management services for college graduates. To organize the recruitment of a set of detailed work procedures, that is, every year before the end of April, various regions collect, collect and report information on the needs of grassroots posts such as rural education, agriculture, health, etc.; Before the end of may each year, the public recruitment will be conducted by means of assessment or examination according to the recruitment plan issued by the local government. Each year before the end of July, send "three support and one help" college students to report to the service unit. The notice has made detailed provisions on household file management, daily management, assessment management and fund guarantee during the working period of "three supports and one assistance" college students. For college students who have passed the examination after the service period, the "service certificate for college graduates to rural grassroots" printed by the ministry of personnel shall be issued as the basis for enjoying relevant preferential employment policies after the service period.

3. Time limit: working time is 2 to 3 years commonly, the life allowance that gives certain when working. After the work period, the independent choice of career, employment period enjoy certain preferential policies.

4. Employment services and preferential policies:

(1) after work expires, if former basic unit serves a unit to have job vacancy, want to give priority to "3 support one help" personnel, the enterprise unit that is located county, town has duty vacancy, also should take out partial duty to absorb this part of graduate.

(2) for those who are prepared to start their own businesses, they can enjoy relevant policies such as administrative fee reduction, small-sum loan guarantee and discount interest.

(3) the graduates who have completed the service period and have passed the assessment can enjoy certain policy bonus points or the same conditions for preferential recruitment.

(4) to serve for more than 2 years in western regions and arduous and remote regions, and to register for the preliminary examination of master graduate students within 3 years after the service expiration, plus 10 points, with priority to be admitted under the same conditions.

(5) "three supports and one assistance" college students who have passed the service examination can return to their original place of origin or work in other areas according to their wishes. If the receiving unit has been implemented, the receiving unit shall be granted a hukou in the region where it is located

(6) upon entering a state-owned enterprise or public institution, the receiving unit shall determine the salary standard for the position according to the personnel with the same conditions. The service term shall be calculated as length of service.
