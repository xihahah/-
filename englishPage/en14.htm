Work-study center honors the general assembly and the 13th general election congress successfully concluded

The work-study center commendation conference and the 13th general election conference were successfully held at the lecture hall of science and technology building of huajiang campus at 14:30 on June 9, 2018. The teachers of the student aid center and the student representatives of the fraternal organizations attended the conference as guests. The conference process is divided into two parts: preparatory meeting and formal meeting

june 2018

The preparatory meeting

The preparatory meeting began, by the work-study center executive vice President huang chunmei announced the official start of the meeting, immediately after the assistantship presidium members read out conference documents. Next, teacher wen xiujun of the student financial aid center gave a speech, affirming the work of the work-study center in the past year, and highly praised the activities held in the past year. Meanwhile, he placed high hopes on the work-study center, hoping that the work-study center would continue to practice the spirit of based on students, challenging themselves and serving the society. With the logistics support chairman liu he wei made the annual work summary report of 2017-2018, summarizing the work of the year.

At the end of the preparatory meeting, the "advanced individual" of the work-study center was commended, and the prize was awarded by the teachers and the assistantship presidium. Thus, the preparatory meeting ended smoothly.

When the formal meeting was held, all the candidates of the center came to the stage and gave their campaign speeches with full enthusiasm and high spirits. They all gave their feelings and prospects of the work in the work-study center family for one year, and showed their determination to run for office. In the end, the official members were elected by vote.

This election conference is in line with the principle of openness, justice and fairness. After voting, the official list of the 13th work-study center committee was finally issued, and the general election conference of work-study center was successfully concluded.

In a hurry that year, the work-study center family and warm through a year. The conference fully recognized the work of the service members over the past year. Looking forward to the future, do not forget the original intention, fang has always, we have confidence and ability, the work-study center is bound to have a more brilliant tomorrow.
