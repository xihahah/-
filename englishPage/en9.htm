The implementation plan of rural support education plan for outstanding retired teachers in guangxi in the 2018-2019 academic year of wuzhou education bureau

2018-07-10 16:50:20 Teachers examination network http://gx.huatu.com/jiaoshi/ Article source:Wuzhou talent net

Wuzhou education bureau 2018-2019 year guangxi outstanding retired teachers rural support education plan implementation plan, detailed information please read the following! If you have any questions, please add [2018 guangxi teachers preparation exchange group: 368346475], for more information, please pay attention to guangxi huatu WeChat public number (gxhuatu), guangxi teachers recruitment examination curriculum consultation phone: 400-9bake 7716, WeChat ID: 17776255380

The education bureau of wuzhou city organized and carried out the implementation plan of rural support education plan for outstanding retired teachers in guangxi in the 2018-2019 academic year

In order to implement the notice of the general office of the people's government of guangxi zhuang autonomous region on printing and distributing the implementation plan of guangxi rural teacher support plan (2015-2020) (guangxi administrative office issued [2015] no. 130)), in the 2018-2019 academic year, zhuhai will continue to organize and implement the rural support education plan for outstanding retired teachers in guangxi. To do a good job in recruiting excellent retired teachers, the following plans are made:

1. Goals of supporting education
　　By facing the public recruiting outstanding rural retired teacher education volunteers to be a teacher in the city poverty county (district), rural school, ease the shortage of rural school teachers, drive the recipient school teachers teaching level overall ascension, promote the balanced development of the compulsory education in poverty-stricken areas, to achieve our goals and tasks of education crucial for poverty alleviation plan.

2. Teaching time
　　The volunteer teaching period is 2018-2019 academic year. In principle, the duration of supporting education shall not be less than one academic year, and excellent retired teachers shall be encouraged to continue supporting education.

3. Place of supporting education
　　The place of supporting education proposed by our city is four counties (districts), including cangwu county, teng county, mengshan county and longdike district. The recipient school is the obligation education school for the town and below (excluding the town where the town is located and the village school under the town).

4. Recruitment
　　According to the regional allocation indicators, a total of 111 volunteer teachers will be recruited in the 2018-2019 academic year, and the recruitment plan for each aided county (district) (see annex 1 for details).

5. Recruitment objects and conditions
　　The recruitment target is the excellent retired primary and secondary school teachers in guangxi zhuang autonomous region. It can also be recruited outside the region, but it must meet the following conditions:

(1) love education career, have good political ideological quality, have a strong sense of career, responsibility and mission, be willing to contribute, volunteer to teach in the countryside.

(2) I am healthy and have been retired for less than 5 years. I am able to undertake classroom teaching and other education teaching work during the volunteer teaching.

(3) education has rich teaching experience, with intermediate and higher titles. Special grade teachers, autonomous region teachers, principals and "bayi teachers" are welcome to participate in the registration.

(4) applicants should also meet other requirements of recruitment positions in their region.

6. Form of supporting education
　　Good retired teachers teaching volunteers can according to their own expertise to carry out the education activity is given priority to with classroom teaching, can also according to the recipient school education teaching needs to listen to the teacher evaluation, common course open, seminar or seminar, guidance of young teachers, to assist the school teaching management and to carry out the research activity of rich variety of teaching activities.

7. Supplementary funds
　　The related matters of subsidy funds shall be implemented in accordance with the notice of regional education department on organizing and carrying out rural support education plan for outstanding retired teachers in guangxi in the 2018-2019 academic year (guangxi normal university [2018] no. 29).

(1) subsidy standard

　　<1> outstanding retired teacher voluntary teaching volunteer carries on the life subsidy in accordance with the standard of 30,000 yuan/school year/person (the school year is calculated according to 10 months).

<2> Around can be combined with the local reality, to get a super teacher, teacher, principal and "BaGui teacher education teaching masters honors such as" excellent retired teachers teaching volunteers, on the basis of the above allowances to increase 05000 yuan/year/person, increase the portion of the funds by recipient county (city, area) form a complete set of fiscal subsidies.

(2) use of funds

In accordance with the spirit of document no. 130 issued by the administrative office of guangxi [2015], the special fund for supporting education and education of the autonomous region and the "silver age teaching plan" in the special plan for teachers under the "three districts" talent support plan issued by the central government are planned to be coordinated in 2018 to start the plan. Special funds will be arranged by the financial departments of each aided county for the recruitment of outstanding retired teachers in rural areas, necessary transportation subsidies and the purchase of personal accident insurance for volunteers.

8. Recruitment process
　　According to the requirements of the documents of the education department of the autonomous region, the recruitment shall be under the responsibility and leadership of the education department of the autonomous region, and shall be implemented by the counties (cities and districts) receiving assistance. Follow the principle of "openness, fairness, voluntariness and meritocracy" and proceed according to the following procedures:

(1)A recruitment notice was issued (10 July). The education department of the autonomous region issued the recruitment notice at the same time on the public information website (http://www.gxedu.gov.cn/), guangxi daily, nanguo morning post and other media. The city, county (city, district) shall publish the recruitment information of the district on relevant websites and local media according to the local actual situation (at the same time publish the list and application form of rural schools and positions to be recruited as volunteer teachers).

(2)Voluntary registration (July 11 solstice July 31). Excellent qualified personnel, a retired teacher can through the above channels refer to recruit information, according to the principle of voluntary, choose teaching area and post, download fill out the "best retired teachers of guangxi rural teaching application form (attachment 2), and attach relevant documents, sign up the recipient in the to the county (city, area) bureau of education qualification audit.

(3)Eligibility review (1 solstice 10 aug). Each aid county (city, district) education bureau to register the personnel material to carry on the examination, determines the interview personnel list. And inform the shortlisted candidates to assess the time and place and other specific matters.

(4)Interview review (aug 13 solstice 17). Candidates who pass the qualification examination shall participate in the interview examination and physical examination organized by the education bureau of the county (city, district) reported by them. According to interview scores, the list of candidates is determined from high to low.

(5)Public announcement (August 20 solstice 24). After the volunteers have passed the interview and physical examination, the education bureau of the counties (cities and districts) receiving aid shall make the list of potential recruits available to the public for no less than 5 working days, and the list shall be published to the society after the public announcement.

(6)Sign the agreement (before August 30). Each county (city, district) education bureau signed the volunteer service agreement for supporting education with the intended recruits (annex 3), which was signed once a year. Clarify the rights and obligations of both parties, including teaching time, supporting schools, supporting teaching methods and contents, working and living conditions, living subsidies, and dispute resolution methods. Before the formal signing, retired teachers should provide medical examination report and application form after checking and stamping for nearly three months.

(7)Take up a teaching post. Before the fall semester starts in September, outstanding retired teachers who signed the agreement are arranged by the education bureau of the aided county (city, district) to teach in the aided school.

9. Organizational management

(1)Clear responsibility and coordination. According to the documents of the education department of the autonomous region, the education department of the autonomous region is responsible for the public recruitment of outstanding retired teachers as a whole, coordination and guidance, and the implementation of subsidy funds. The municipal education bureau is responsible for the supervision and control of supporting education, and organizing and coordinating the supporting education work of each county under its jurisdiction. The county education bureau is specifically responsible for the recruitment and management of volunteers for supporting education in the region, and formulates the implementation rules for the recruitment of "excellent retired teachers' rural supporting education volunteers" in the county, specifically responsible for the recruitment, physical examination, recruitment, agreement signing, and volunteer management within the service period.

(2)We will strengthen oversight and inspection and implement a work reporting system. Recipient county (city, area) to strengthen the inspection guide regional volunteer work, seriously listen to the views of education both volunteers and beneficiary schools and requirements, coordinate to solve the problems existing in the teaching work, timely accumulation program in the process of information collection, summary of work experience, outstanding, a retired teacher of country assistance will work teaching implementation report at the end of each term to the autonomous region education department, at the same time submit the city bureau of education personnel section.

10. Safeguard policy

(1)During the period of volunteer teaching, the counties (cities and districts) receiving assistance shall organize the follow-up evaluation. For those who fail to perform their obligations as required by the agreement, or are not suitable to continue supporting education due to physical reasons, the agreement shall be terminated and corresponding subsidy fees shall be recovered in proportion to time.

(2)The education bureau of the counties (cities and districts) receiving aid shall actively study and formulate policies and measures to encourage volunteer teachers to continue to support poor rural areas after the one-year service period expires. Volunteer teachers who intend to continue to serve as volunteers can renew the volunteer service agreement next year if relevant conditions are met.

(3)during the service, the volunteer of supporting education does not transfer the hukou.

(4)the relevant departments of the counties (cities and districts) receiving aid shall provide necessary conditions for the volunteers to work, live and be safe, so as to facilitate their work and life.
