Subsidy standards for rural teachers in xinjiang, salary treatment and subsidy program for rural teachers in xinjiang

Kashi region, hetian region, education bureau of kizilsu kirghi autonomous prefecture, and finance bureau:

Treatment of differential policies for the implementation of rural teachers, improve the rural teachers' living wage, further stabilize rural teachers, autonomous decision, since January, in the autonomous region of primary and secondary schools and kindergarten teachers enjoy subsidies township and village cadres at the grass-roots level, on the basis of the concentrated poverty-striken county rural teachers' living subsidies policy to further improve. For this reason, the department of education and the department of finance of the autonomous region have worked out the measures to further improve the living subsidies for rural teachers in xinjiang uygur autonomous region.

Please related (state) education bureau, bureau of finance according to the xinjiang uygur autonomous region to further perfect the measures for the implementation of concentrated poverty-striken county rural teachers' living subsidies (try out) ", as soon as possible to formulate local rural teachers living subsidies implementation scheme and rural subsistence allowances, budgets, submitted to the teacher's essence, departments of educational, scientific and cultural autonomous region department of education.

In the future, the budget of living subsidy for rural teachers should be submitted before August 10th of the previous year.

Contact number: 0991-7606121

Xinjiang uygur autonomous region to further improve the implementation method of living subsidy for rural teachers in contiguous county (trial)

For the implementation of the "the central committee of the communist party of China, the state council on accelerating the development of modern agriculture to further enhance the vitality of rural development certain opinions" (found (2013) no. 1) and the ministry of education of the ministry of finance on the implementation of 2013 central no. 1 files required to work in the continuous destitute areas in rural subsistence allowances, teacher give notice (teach letter [2013] no. 106) spirit, vigorously strengthen the construction of rural teachers, improve the rural teachers' treatment, in the autonomous region of primary and secondary schools and kindergarten teachers enjoy subsidies township and village cadres at the grass-roots level, on the basis of the concentrated poverty-striken county rural teachers' living subsidies to adjust. In order to complete the policy adjustment, the following measures are formulated.

1. guiding ideology and objectives

By the state council on strengthening the construction of teachers' opinions "(guo fa [2012] no. 41) and the ministry of education, the public, the national development and reform commission, ministry of finance, human resources and social security ministry about promote the building of rural compulsory education teachers opinions (teachers [2012] 9) spirit as a guide, through to the rural teachers' living subsidies policy implementation, improve the rural teachers treatment, alleviate rural teachers recruitment KongGang rate is high, the loss of quantity shortage, serious" problem, stable rural teachers' team, ensure that rural teachers "recruit to come, have to go to, to retain and a better teacher". We will ensure that rural school teachers are paid more than urban and rural teachers, and rural teachers in remote and poor areas are paid more than rural teachers in other areas, so as to achieve the goal of "improving the status and treatment of rural teachers and increasing their occupational attractiveness".

2. Scope of subsidy

In view of the fact that the working and living conditions of rural teachers in the three prefectures of southern xinjiang are particularly difficult, the autonomous region, on the basis of the current policy of subsidizing rural cadres at the grass-roots level, has raised the living allowances of rural teachers in 24 counties and cities of the three prefectures of southern xinjiang. The specific scope is as follows:

Kashgar region: kashgar city, shufu county, shule county, yingjisha county, zepu county, shaka county, yagi county, yaecheng county, yuepuhu county, jiashi county, bachu county, tashkurgan tajik autonomous county;

Hetian region: hetian city, hetian county, moyu county, pishan county, lopu county, kele county, yutian county, minfeng county;

Kezile sukirgizi autonomous prefecture: atushi city, aktun county, aheqi county, ucha county.

3. Objects and conditions of subsidy

Rural public primary and middle schools and kindergartens in the southern xinjiang sandizhou rural area (including special post teachers within the service period of three years), specifically refers to the southern xinjiang sandizhou urban areas, counties outside the rural teachers.

The following persons do not enjoy living subsidies for rural teachers:

(1) the grade of the last year's year-end assessment is unqualified for on-job teachers;

(2) teachers who are warned, demerit, demoted or removed from professional and technical posts for violation of professional ethics and political discipline;

(3) absent teachers (including absent teachers due to secondment, transfer, long-term medical leave, etc., except those who are out on duty);

(4) retired teachers;

(5) teachers who support education in rural schools but have been subsidized by the "special plan for teachers under the talent plan of the three regions" of the autonomous region.

4. Implementation principles of subsidies

(1) it shall be paid on a monthly basis. Life allowance is decided according to post, every work on post of rural school (kindergarten) job teacher can enjoy, and life allowance is paid by the month. Those who leave the post of rural school (kindergarten) will cancel the living allowance from the next month.

(2) distinguish standards and highlight key points. When providing living subsidies, we should always give preferential treatment to schools (kindergartens), small villages and teaching centers in remote townships, towns with difficult conditions, and mountainous townships. The autonomous regional finance will allocate funds to counties and cities according to the standard of 200 yuan per teacher per month. In formulating the standard of living allowance, counties and cities do not adopt equalitarianism, and factors such as the distance of transportation and the hardship of living conditions of local rural teachers should be taken into account comprehensively. In principle, standards should be divided into three levels: 300 yuan, 200 yuan and 100 yuan.

(3) open and fair, sunshine operation. When implementing living subsidies, subsistence allowances, scope, object, conditions, grade standard, through the local education department, newspapers, websites, and facilitate the public knows the way, such as school public bar according to the annual fair show, and set up the superintendent to report telephone and online report mailbox, to accept the complaints from teachers, the whole process of supervision by the society.

5. Sources and allocation of subsidy funds

The funds needed for rural teachers' living allowances shall be coordinated by the central and autonomous regional governments. The autonomous region government will allocate funds for rural teachers' living allowances to counties and cities, which will be arranged in line with local conditions.

6. Requirements

(1) the financial department of the three prefectures of southern xinjiang should pay high attention to, strengthen organization and leadership earnestly, formulate specific implementation plans based on the actual situation, and report to the department of education and department of finance of the autonomous region for the record.

(2) for rural teachers who enjoy living subsidies, the annual statistics of teacher real name system in autonomous region shall prevail, and the real name system shall be administered. Rural teachers living subsidies shall be directly distributed to the personal pay card account of teachers.

(3) strengthen the propaganda work of rural teachers' living subsidy policy, make full use of local media, adopt various ways, actively publicize the policy, strengthen the guidance of public opinion, and strive to create a good atmosphere for the whole society to pay attention to rural education and care about rural teachers.

(4) strengthen the management and supervision of rural teachers' living subsidy funds, so as to achieve special use of special funds. After the financial department of the autonomous region allocated the subsidy funds to the county and city, the county and city shall allocate the subsidy funds to the teachers in a timely manner, and shall not be detained, occupied or misappropriated for any reason, so as to prevent the occurrence of false report, false claim and withdrawal of subsidy funds. The department of education and the department of finance of the autonomous region will carry out an irregular inspection on the use of special funds in various regions. For counties (cities) that fail to implement the rural teachers' living subsidy policy according to the requirements, or that are detained, occupied or misappropriated special funds, special funds will be recovered or deducted according to the situation, and criticism will be reported.

(5) before the end of January each year, relevant prefectural education and financial department shall report the implementation of rural teachers' living subsidies to the education department and finance department of autonomous region through the "information reporting platform for rural teachers' living subsidies".

(6) pay attention to improving the supporting mechanism of rural teachers' team construction. We should take comprehensive measures to improve the quality of rural teachers. Teacher training should be inclined to rural teachers and improve their participation rate. It is necessary to establish the rotation and exchange mechanism for teachers in counties (cities and districts) to promote the scientific and orderly flow of teachers. We should improve the system of supporting rural schools by urban school teachers, and regularly send urban teachers to rural schools as volunteers. The evaluation of teacher's position (title) is inclined to the rural and remote school teachers. We will effectively increase the proportion of senior teachers' professional titles in schools located in towns and villages and below, and schools in cities and towns shall not occupy posts of senior teachers' professional titles in schools located in towns and villages and below. Urban primary and middle school teachers should have more than one year of teaching experience in rural schools or weak schools when evaluating senior positions.

(7) these measures shall be implemented from January.

(8) the present measures shall be interpreted by the department of education and the department of finance of the autonomous region.
