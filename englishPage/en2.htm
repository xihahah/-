Leaders of hubei university of biotechnology vocational college led a team to visit students of supporting education in xuanen and carry out poverty alleviation research

2018-07-09 Source: window to Chinese universities

From July 1 to 3, zhou yuancai, a member of the party committee of hubei vocational college of biological science and technology, led a delegation of six persons, including the department of science and technology, animal (food) college of science and technology, and the department of horticulture and gardening. Hu mingchen, head of the provincial department of agriculture in xuanen county, accompanied the visiting and research.

The research group visited seven students of hubei biological technology vocational college in three supporting schools, namely tan jiaping primary school, xiping primary school and xuanen county secondary vocational and technical school, and held talks with the relevant leaders of local education department and supporting students. They gave each student an e-paper book on behalf of the school. During the discussion with the students, zhou yuancai and the teachers inquired about their teaching, study and life in detail, and expressed their admiration for their devotion to work, which is rooted in the countryside, caring for the students, careful teaching, and modest and eager to learn. The students told about their life in the past 9 months, expressing their love for the front line of rural teaching and welcoming more students from junior to participate in the teaching activities. Their stories touched the teachers and leaders present at the scene.

The visiting research group and the county leaders in charge of education in xuanen county, as well as the relevant persons in charge of the county education bureau and the county human society bureau held a discussion in the meeting room of the county government. The two sides exchanged views on supporting education in poverty alleviation.
