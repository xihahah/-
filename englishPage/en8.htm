Henan province to help xinjiang ten thousand teachers to support the implementation plan came

2018-07-13 09:05 Henan provincial department of education There have been 580 readings

To fully implement the party's 19 big spirit, in-depth implementation of xi jinping, the new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics, according to the ministry of education of the national development and reform committee, ministry of finance issued by the ministry of human resources and social security about xinjiang < Tibet with teacher teaching plan implementation plan > notice [2017] no. 14 (teachers) spirit, focus on high quality education resources in our province to send xinjiang teachers in xinjiang, and training the local teachers, help the xinjiang overall improving the quality of education, to be completed for win out of poverty, achieve peace and stability of xinjiang to provide strong protection. The following work plan is made for the implementation of xinjiang support education plan in our province.
1. work objectives

Through xinjiang teacher teaching plan, set up with (including thirteen regiment, hereinafter the same) to share with the school in the province the normalized mechanism of high quality education resources, alleviate the contradiction of insufficient with good teacher, also assisted the school education teaching level, strengthen the xinjiang hami teachers team construction, improve local education self ability, comprehensive improve the quality of ShouYuanDe foundation education, for economic development, social stability and security in xinjiang, cultivate patriotic love parties, qualified builders and reliable successors to the cause of socialism.
2. Main tasks

According to the national Tibet by xinjiang teacher teaching plan, combine with demand and our province reality, according to the principle of "rational, sustainable and achievable", starting from 2018, further intensify assistance, from all the xinjiang city of the province, and selected a total of 125 outstanding teachers (including full-time teachers and education administrators), with 70 people, the corps of 13 t 55 people to be a teacher in xinjiang. According to the needs of the recipient areas, science teachers should be the main ones and other subjects should be taken into account in principle. We will give priority to secondary school teachers and give consideration to primary school students. We will encourage a group to support one school. The specific selection plan and discipline requirements are connected by the education department of hami city of xinjiang and henan province, and the selection plan is determined through consultation by both parties (see annex 1 for details). The full-time teachers in xinjiang are mainly responsible for subject teaching and class management, organizing teaching and research activities, conducting business training and teaching guidance, giving full play to the role of backbone demonstration, forming a teaching team with local teachers, promoting the overall improvement of science teaching level and education management ability of the aided schools. Education management personnel in xinjiang are mainly engaged in school management work, to promote the comprehensive improvement of education teaching and management level. The aided schools shall organize teachers, administrators and supporting teachers to work together.
3.Selection requirements

(1)According to the assignment of tasks, teachers should be selected precisely for supporting education in xinjiang. In principle, teachers should be selected from primary and middle schools with high level of education teaching management. In a city with a large number of school assignments or difficulties, new teachers and excellent teachers who have retired in the past three years can be selected.

(2)The teachers sent to xinjiang are required to be reliable in politics, noble in ethics, devoted to work and excellent in business, resolutely carry out the party's ethnic and religious policies, and safeguard the reunification of the motherland and national unity. Have strong working ability and sense of responsibility; A solid style of work, not afraid of hardship, dedication, health; In addition to the above requirements, school administrators should also have strong organizational leadership and rich school management experience. No units may select teachers from among the redirected or laid off persons to assist xinjiang.
Aid xinjiang teachers age unlimited. Before the volunteer teachers enter xinjiang, the municipal education bureau will arrange the targeted physical examination.

(3)In 2018, the time for selecting xinjiang teachers to assist xinjiang was one and a half years. In spring 2020, the time for assisting xinjiang was adjusted to 2-3 years according to the teaching rules and task demands, and the number of students who volunteered for xinjiang remained unchanged. If it is necessary to replace personnel in special circumstances during the period of supporting education, it shall be determined by the aid recipients through consultation, and be supplemented in a timely manner to ensure the continuity of teaching management. To encourage the extension of volunteer teaching time or work in xinjiang.

(4)The school that support education limits is the school that town of county or central town built turnover dormitory, the area that gets aid, unit is in support education school provides necessary traffic, life facility for the teacher that support education.
4.Management style

(1)During the period of working in xinjiang, the teachers sent to xinjiang were jointly managed by the dispatched units and hami city, and mainly managed by the party committees (party groups) at all levels in hami city. The party committee (party group) of each aided district and unit and its personnel department, education department and the provincial forward headquarters of xinjiang aid are responsible for the daily management and service of the teachers in xinjiang.

(2)All departments concerned in hami city of xinjiang should regard xinjiang teachers as an important part of the construction of talent team in the region and the unit, and give full trust in politics, support in work, care passionately in life and strict requirements in management. We should change the concept of "taking on posts", and create conditions for teachers in xinjiang to play the role of "teaching guide", so as to ensure that teachers supporting education play the role of backbone demonstration in suitable positions. Party committees (party groups) at or above the county level in each region shall assume the responsibility of ensuring safety during the supporting education period for teachers in xinjiang, strengthen communication and contact with teachers in xinjiang and dispatched units, and timely report relevant information.
5.Policy treatment

(1)The relevant treatment of xinjiang teachers can be implemented in accordance with the relevant provisions of "notice > of the ministry of human resources and social security of the organization department of the central committee of the communist party of China on the issuance of regulations on the administration of counterpart support for xinjiang cadres and talents" (group general character [2011] no. 6). The funds required for the selection of teachers are included in the provincial plan of counterpart support for xinjiang's economic and social development, and the subsidy funds for xinjiang teachers are listed in the special fund for counterpart support xinjiang according to the document no. 55 of yujiantong [2010].

(2)During the period of xinjiang aid, the personnel relationship remained in the original work unit, and after the period of xinjiang aid, returned to the original work unit. During the period of xinjiang aid, wages shall be paid by the original work unit, which shall enjoy various welfare treatment for the same type and level personnel of the original work unit. Aid xinjiang teachers also enjoy the xinjiang region similar to the same level of hard and remote areas subsidies.

(3)Hami city according to the provisions for each volunteer teachers to purchase the personal accident insurance xinjiang aid, every year to organize xinjiang teachers in xinjiang physical examination. The medical treatment charge that produces because cause injury, press him medical treatment concern and concerned regulation are dealt with. Aid xinjiang teachers in accordance with the relevant provisions to enjoy the normal family visits and summer holidays, do not participate in the activities and learning in the summer and winter vacation organized by the area. In the absence of leave, the recipient may reimburse the transportation expenses of a family member in and out of the recipient area in accordance with the relevant provisions.

(4)The work experience of teachers in xinjiang is regarded as the same as that of urban teachers to teach at the rural grassroots level. When applying for and evaluating the professional title (post), teachers in xinjiang are given priority. Qualified teachers (excluding senior teachers) who have met the standard conditions may be promoted to the first grade in accordance with the procedure and the evaluation committee. During the period of xinjiang aid, those who meet the conditions for the evaluation and appointment of a higher level of professional title in the place of aid can participate in the evaluation and appointment of professional title in xinjiang, which does not account for the number of school titles. After returning to the original selected units after the period of xinjiang aid, those who pass the evaluation should be appointed according to the professional technical posts and posts in xinjiang under the premise of the proportion and scale of the professional technical posts in the original units. After the expiration of the period of aid to xinjiang, if the original unit was abolished, merged or restructured, the city and unit dispatched to take charge of proper arrangements for their work.

(5)The annual assessment of xinjiang teachers shall be undertaken by the recipient units, and the assessment results shall be copied to the sending units. The application of assessment results should be strengthened and the correct orientation should be established. For outstanding teachers who performed well and were popular at the grassroots level during the period of xinjiang aid, xinjiang should give priority to evaluation and commendation. Those who have made outstanding contributions may be commended and rewarded in accordance with relevant regulations after they have solicited the opinions of the personnel department of the sending units in xinjiang. Those who are incompetent in assessment or have problems during the period of aid to xinjiang shall be dealt with in accordance with relevant regulations.
6.To organize and implement

(1)The provincial department of education, the national development and reform commission, the department of finance, the department of human resources and social security and other departments have established a mechanism of guidance, coordination, tracking and supervision based on their functions, and jointly organized and implemented the plan of supporting 10,000 teachers in xinjiang.

(2)The provincial department of education organized relevant personnel to go to xinjiang to receive xinjiang work. By the end of July, the provincial cities shall be instructed to complete the selection of teachers to assist xinjiang, and the training tasks for teachers to assist xinjiang in August shall be completed. The first batch of selected teachers for xinjiang will be in place before the school begins in autumn 2018.

(3)In order to solve practical difficulties for teachers in xinjiang and create favorable conditions for them, the cities should coordinate closely with each other in terms of their functions, formulate specific implementation plans, do a good job in personnel selection, implement relevant funds and incentive policies.

(4)The municipal education bureau is requested to confirm a liaison officer for supporting education in xinjiang and submit the list of teachers to the provincial education department normal office before July 30 (see annex 2). We will inform you about the assistance to schools in xinjiang.
