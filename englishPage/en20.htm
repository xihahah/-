Notification of xiamen municipal education bureau on the selection of teachers to Tibet, xinjiang and ningxia in 2018

May 28, 2018 Source: xiamen education bureau

Notice on the selection of teachers to Tibet, xinjiang and ningxia in 2018

Education bureaus and directly affiliated schools in each district:

The fujian province education department according to the fujian province department of education office about to do a good job of teaching in teachers selected 2018 Tibet notice (fujian administrative division [2018] no. 23), the office of fujian province department of education about send to be a teacher in ningxia group of 20 teachers notice (fujian administrative division [2018] no. 24), the office of fujian province department of education about ready to be a teacher in xinjiang in 2018 teachers selected work notice (fujian administrative division [2018] no. 25) file request, This year, 26 teachers from the whole city are required to be sent to zuogong county, qamdo city, Tibet, ningxia and xinjiang to carry out volunteer teaching. The related matters are hereby notified as follows:

1. Task allocation

Five teachers from the whole city were sent to zuogong county, changdu city, Tibet, four to ningxia and 17 to xinjiang for volunteer teaching. The specific quota allocation is as follows:

2. Selection criteria

(1) Tibetan teachers in principle from the management level of higher education teaching primary and secondary schools send, send the teachers should have in principle the intermediate and above titles, age generally under 45 one full year of life, healthy body, the demand political reliability, high ethics, wuxi, sophisticated business, firmly implement the party's ethnic and religious policy, maintaining the reunification of the motherland and national unity; Have strong working ability and sense of responsibility; A solid style, not afraid of hardship, willing to dedication.

(2) the teachers who go to ningxia for volunteer teaching require good political thinking, decent style and good health. In principle, they should have more than three years of teaching experience, and they are preferred to recommend backbone teachers with intermediate or higher professional titles.

(3) xinjiang teachers in principle from the higher education teaching management level of small and medium-sized school selected, select teachers should teach more than 3 years in principle, with strong education teaching ability and rich experience in teaching, politics, noble ethics, wuxi, reliable business is excellent, firmly implement the party's ethnic and religious policy, maintaining the reunification of the motherland and national unity; Have strong working ability and sense of responsibility; A solid work style, not afraid of hardship, willing to dedication, health. No supporting teachers shall be selected from the redirected or laid off persons.

3. Duration of supporting education

The volunteer teacher sent in this school will be from July to August 2018. The volunteer teacher will be sent in Tibet and xinjiang for one and a half years. Ningxia 1 year.

4. Treatment of supporting teachers

(1) the policy and treatment of teachers in Tibet shall be handled according to the document no. 23 issued by the education office of fujian [2018]. That is, in addition to the execution in accordance with the document issued by fujian education personnel [2016] no. 16, the following policy treatment can be enjoyed;

<1> assisting teachers meet the requirements of senior teachers' professional title application. The professional title of senior teachers is not subject to the restrictions of school positions.

<2> during the period of working in Tibet, the municipal education bureau provided a living allowance of 2,000 yuan per student per month, except that the provincial finance provided a fixed living allowance per student per month.

<3> during the period of aid to Tibet, the class teacher received an annual evaluation award of 5000 yuan.

<4> the annual performance appraisal of the school shall be deemed as excellent.

(2) the treatment of the volunteer teachers in ningxia shall be handled according to the no. 24 of fujian education office (2018).

(3) the supporting education teachers in xinjiang should be handled according to no. 25 of fujian education office (2018).

5. Selection requirements

Selecting excellent teachers to support education is a political task of implementing the counterpart support work of the CPC central committee and the state council. The education bureau of each district, concerned school should attach great importance to, elaborate organization, adopt the form of combination of individual voluntary registration and organization recommendation to undertake, encourage teacher to register actively actively, do the work of registering and recommending earnestly.

According to the time arrangement of provincial office, each district education bureau, each school must put forward the preliminary candidate before June 20 and fill in the recommendation summary form and the recommendation form (in 3 copies, including electronic version) and submit to the personnel office of the municipal education bureau.
