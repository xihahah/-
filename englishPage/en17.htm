Jiangxi: students of yin-ti mei normal university will go to the countryside to practice and teach

2018-07-06 19:17:33 Source: xinhuanet

Nanchang, July 6 (xinhua) -- starting from the autumn of 2018, jiangxi province will expand the scope of supporting education for students majoring in phonetic beauty teachers in colleges and universities to 1360 students from 90 counties.

According to jiangxi provincial department of education, college students who participate in the internship can enjoy the provincial financial arrangement of 600 yuan per month living subsidies, and 600 yuan per semester one-time transportation subsidies policy, college students who support education will also give a certain amount of supporting subsidies. At the same time, the internship experience of supporting education for college students can be regarded as education internship experience, which can be first employed under the same conditions in the recruitment of primary and secondary school teachers in jiangxi province.

In the autumn of 2017, jiangxi province launched the pilot project of supporting education for students majoring in phonetic beauty in colleges and universities in remote rural primary schools, recruiting 180 students from 9 normal colleges and universities in the province, and arranging 60 remote rural primary schools in 30 state-level poverty-stricken counties and contiguous poverty-stricken counties for six months.
