What is a volunteer teacher

Supporting education refers to the education and teaching management of primary and secondary schools in villages and towns in backward areas, which is mainly divided into supporting education for half a year and more than half a year, supporting education for one to three months, and supporting education for winter and summer holidays. In the broad sense, supporting education is to improve local education environment and cultivate local teachers. Support education in the narrow sense is to go to the field of support education behavior.

History and development history of supporting education industry

Organized and team-oriented supporting education institutions have a history of no more than 20 years in China. Individual nature of the start of supporting education activities In the mid to late 1980s. In 1999, the graduate student support group organized by the central committee of the communist youth league and the ministry of education was undoubtedly the earliest official Party support education activities. The graduate student support group has grown from 101 students from 22 universities to 187 in 2017 2043 people. A total of 16,196 students were sent to teach for graduate students, and their service areas were developed from seven counties in five provinces in 1999 To 295 counties in 19 provinces in 2017.

What conditions should I have to take part in teaching

The activities of supporting education for college students have always been popular in colleges and universities, and even supporting education is the dream of some college students. Volunteer teaching activities can not only exercise themselves to experience life, but also help poor areas to give their love, which has always been the first choice for college students in holidays. As students of normal major, in the future job application process, the volunteer teaching experience can also be used as an advantage to compete to win the favor of employers. We should not miss such a meaningful activity, what kind of conditions do we need to take part in the volunteer teaching activity? The following is a detailed explanation for you.

Good quality and appropriate level of knowledge

Since it is to support education, to help students in poor areas, to teach them cultural knowledge and new things, to open closed vision for them. So the premise of teaching others is that you have to be a high quality person. For example, if you teach an English course, you can't take all the children with you if you speak chinglish. Therefore, the guarantee of your professional level is the prerequisite for you to support education.
