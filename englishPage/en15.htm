Youth support education, building dreams

Guidian youth association May 27

Green education & 2018/5/4

"Love makes dreams come true, we are with you"

At 12 o 'clock at noon, our volunteers rushed to lunch after class and got on the bus to teach. Although the weather is hot and the sun is hot, we have no less enthusiasm for supporting education. Because the little faces in my mind, holding our hands lovingly, shouted, brothers and sisters - the images of you coming, let us hope to see them again~

Let's take a look at the happy time that children and volunteers spent together~

At 1 PM, we arrived at our destination - ling tian primary school.

Time and again, we have further integrated into this small group. We are inspired by their every move and every growth.

Basketball class

I heard the guys who play basketball seriously are very handsome~

Only in the field can we find the most innocent happiness, enjoy the feeling of the perspiration across the face, all the unhappiness into muddy perspiration! See the children's love for basketball, all let me ignite the love for basketball.

Sign language class

Let love speak in different ways

Let love spread to every corner of the world

The silent wins the sound, the simple movement has the different significance. Sign language is a unique way of caring for others. Learning more than one language gives you more opportunities to communicate with others.

The little teachers are teaching and the children are studying.

The sketch class

Make a portrait of your mood with a rainbow, it will be more beautiful than ever

Take a picture of your happiness with the vernal wind

Sketching is not copying, but highly creative. And children's creative thinking is pioneering, flexibility. Thoughts leap on the paper, happy buried in the bottom of my heart.

Time flies we this time the activities will end in a twinkling of an eye, respectively, the kids screaming to volunteer's signature, only to leave the footprint of the volunteers, so we decided to pose to record this joyful and meaningful in the afternoon, every lovely face imprint on our photo, at the moment, the picture is not only a photo, it is our friendship together with children, it is eternal!

At 15:10, we sat on the bus, but the children in my mind. This departure is only temporary, our support education activities are still to be continued, look forward to your participation oh~
