Teaching in origin

At the end of 1993, the central committee of the communist youth league decided to implement China's youth volunteer action. On December 19, more than 20,000 railway young people took the lead in carrying out the banner of "young volunteers", carrying out warm volunteer service for passengers along the beijing-guangzhou railway line, marking the beginning of China's large-scale volunteer service.

To further promote the development of youth volunteer service, the central committee of the communist youth league established the China youth volunteer association on December 5, 1994。

In August 1998, the youth volunteer action guidance center of the central committee of the communist youth league was formally established. Its main responsibility is to plan, coordinate and guide the youth volunteer service work of the communist youth league.

By 2000, China had initially formed a network of volunteer service organizations composed of national associations, 36 provincial-level associations, and more than 2/3 local (municipal) associations and some county-level associations. One year after the large-scale development of youth volunteer service in China, the unbalanced development of education in China began to receive attention from youth volunteer service.

In 1994, the central propaganda department, the ministry of education, the mass of the central joint implementation was carried out by college student volunteers summer culture, science and technology, health "volunteer" activities, organizing each year millions of college student volunteers grass-roots level at the grass-roots level and the affected areas, giving full play to the advantages of their knowledge intelligence, carried out the rich contents, various forms of literacy, culture, science and technology, and health services. Among them, the cultural activities of "supporting education" for college and secondary school students in the summer vacation, as well as the grass-roots anti-illiteracy activities, can be regarded as the beginning of the large-scale volunteer supporting education for college students in China.
