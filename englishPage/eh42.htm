The first stage

From 1996 to 1999, it was the first period of rapid development of supporting education for college students in China. During this period, the Chinese government and the central committee of the communist youth league jointly promulgated a series of supporting education policies and carried out a series of supporting education activities, which led to the first upsurge of supporting education for Chinese college students..

In 1996, the communist youth league central at the central civilization office, ministry of education, the ministry of health, ministry of science and technology, the ministry of agriculture, ministry of personnel, the western development office of the state council and other departments, began to pilot "youth volunteer relay for poverty alleviation plan", through open recruitment, regular rotation, insist for a long time relay mechanism, organization to mobilize the youth volunteer issue for poor areas to provide six months to two years of basic education, health care, agricultural science and technology promotion services.

In 1999, the central committee of the communist youth league and the ministry of education jointly organized and implemented the "postgraduate support group", a Chinese youth volunteer poverty alleviation relay program. From 1999 to 2007, nine graduate support groups have been successfully established successively. From 1996 to 1999, the Chinese government and the central committee of the communist youth league jointly promulgated a series of supporting education policies and carried out supporting education activities. The second stage

From 2003 to 2005, it was the second rapid development period of volunteer teaching for college students in China. During this period, the Chinese government and the central committee of the communist youth league issued two major policies, which pushed the support education for college students to the second climax.

In 2003, the communist youth league central committee and the ministry of education, ministry of finance, ministry of personnel of the notice on the implementation of western college students volunteer service plan (middle mediatek [2003126) regulation, according to the open recruitment, voluntary application, organized selection, centralized dispatching approach, to recruit 5000-6000 ordinary institutions of higher learning graduates, in the western poverty-stricken villages and towns in the period of l_2 to education, health, agricultural extension, poverty alleviation, and youth center construction and management of volunteer work. Once the policy is put forward, to cause the attention of the masses of college student volunteers, In addition, an upsurge of college students' volunteer teaching in the west and service in the west was set off in China, and the concept of college students' volunteer teaching began to be formally formed. College students' volunteer teaching began to become one of the popular words in the current society, and began to receive more and more attention and attention from the society.

In 2005, for the implementation of the general office of the general office of the central committee of the CCP, the state council about the opinions of the guide and encourage college graduates to the grass-roots employment "hair (2005) in the (18), the central organization department, ministry of personnel, the ministry of education, ministry of finance, the ministry of agriculture, ministry of health, poverty relief office of the state council, the communist youth league central decision, organized to carry out the college graduates to the grass-roots grass-roots engaged in teaching, agriculture, health and poverty alleviation work, referred to as the" three helps "plan. The plan calls for recruiting 20, 000 college graduates a year for five consecutive years starting from 2006, according to the methods of open recruitment, voluntary registration, organization selection and unified dispatch. The proposal of "three supports and one support" policy is the first time for China to make a clear statement on supporting education for college students in national policy, which also indicates that "supporting education for college students" has been recognized and supported by the Chinese government.
