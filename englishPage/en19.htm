Preferential treatment and policies for 10,000 teachers in Tibet and xinjiang

Source: nanfang daily January 1, 2018

In terms of the selection requirements of the four departments for supporting education teachers in xinjiang, the plan points out that in principle, schools in the provinces and cities where assistance to Tibet is the main subject, with science teachers as the main subject and other disciplines taken into account. A working group of 10-20 teachers will be formed to support one school. Teachers in Tibet are generally under the age of 45 years old, healthy and able to adapt to the plateau working environment. Aid xinjiang teachers age unlimited.

In terms of encouraging policies and preferential treatment, the head of the department of teachers' work of the ministry of education introduced that during the period of aid to Tibet and xinjiang, the salary of the former work unit would be paid by the former work unit, and they would enjoy all kinds of welfare treatment for the same type and level personnel of the former work unit. At the same time, teachers assisted in Tibet shall be provided with allowances according to the local salary standards (including subsidies) for the same level of personnel in Tibet, and teachers assisted in xinjiang shall also enjoy the hardship and remote areas allowance for the same level of personnel in xinjiang. The teachers who work in southern xinjiang enjoy the southern work subsidy according to the regulations. Provinces and cities that provide aid to Tibet can give appropriate living allowances to teachers who assist Tibet in xinjiang according to the existing subsidy standards for cadres and talents.

Aid to Tibet teachers in the evaluation of professional titles by the tilt. According to the above person, the work experience of teachers in Tibet and xinjiang is similar to that of urban teachers to teach at rural grassroots level, and the accumulated work experience of them is the qualification for them to apply for and evaluate their professional titles. When carrying out job title review, should give priority to tilt. Qualified teachers (excluding senior teachers) who have met the standard conditions may be promoted to the first grade in accordance with the procedure and the evaluation committee.

The places receiving aid in Tibet and xinjiang shall purchase personal accident insurance for each volunteer teacher during the period of aid to Tibet, and organize the teachers receiving aid to Tibet to have a physical examination in xinjiang every year. Aid to Tibet teachers in accordance with the relevant provisions of the recipient area to enjoy the normal family visits and summer and winter vacation. In the absence of leave, the recipient may reimburse the transportation expenses of a family member in and out of the recipient area in accordance with the relevant provisions.
