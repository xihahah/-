Notice on selecting the first batch of "assistance Tibet aid xinjiang ten thousand teachers support education plan" supporting teachers

Source: Information availability date：2018-05-22

Education bureau, development and reform commission, finance bureau, human resources and social security bureau:

To fully implement the party's 19 big spirit, in-depth implementation of xi jinping, the new era of socialism with Chinese characteristics, according to the ministry of education and other four departments about print and distribute < Tibet by xinjiang with teacher teaching plan implementation plan > notice [2017] no. 14 (teachers) requirement, will send outstanding teachers in our province to be a teacher in Tibet, xinjiang, drive and training the local teachers, help promote education development of Tibet, xinjiang overall level. The organization department of the provincial party committee is now notified as follows.

1. work objectives

Through the implementation of the country "Tibet by xinjiang than teacher teaching plan", set up Tibet, xinjiang (including corps, similarly hereinafter) Shared with the school in the province the normalized mechanism of high quality education resources, good teachers should play the role of mentoring, stimulate vitality in our province and Tibet, xinjiang teachers as a whole, to help Tibet, comprehensively improve the level of basic education and vocational education development in xinjiang, to Tibet, xinjiang economic development, social stability and security to provide strong protection.

2. Assignment

（1)In accordance with the principle of "reasonable, sustainable and achievable" and according to the national "first batch aid Tibet 10,000 teachers support education plan selection table", the first batch of 485 outstanding teachers (including full-time teachers and education management personnel) were sent to Tibet and xinjiang to support education. In principle, we should give priority to science teachers and give consideration to other disciplines. Middle school teachers to give attention to the primary school and other sections. The time of Tibet in xinjiang is generally about one and a half years. The school that support education limits is county town or the school that center town builds turnover dormitory, do not arrange to the school that elevates 4000 meters above commonly teach.

This time, the supporting education work should give full play to the leading role of group-type supporting education and teaching team. Generally, no less than 5 teachers are arranged for each aided school. Based on the needs of Tibet and xinjiang, the specific selection plan and post requirements of the first batch of districts and cities are determined (see annex 1 for details).

(2)The full-time teachers are mainly responsible for subject teaching and class management, organizing teaching and research activities, conducting business training and teaching guidance, giving full play to the role of backbone demonstration, forming a teaching team with teachers in the places where the aid is received, promoting the overall improvement of the school's teaching level and education management ability.

(3)management personnel of supporting education education are mainly engaged in school management, promoting the assisted schools to comprehensively improve education teaching and management.

3.Selection requirements

(1)Each district must from the education teaching management level high school chooses the support teacher. The number that chooses school is more or have difficulty really, after approved by provincial education department, can choose new teacher, retired teacher.

(2)The selected supporting teachers are required to be politically reliable, morally noble, devoted and professionally competent, safeguard the reunification of the motherland and national unity, and resolutely implement the party's national and religious policies. Have strong working ability and sense of responsibility; The style is solid, not afraid of hardship, dare to devote, healthy. In addition to the above requirements, school administrators should also have strong organizational leadership and rich school management experience. No supporting teachers shall be selected from the redirected or laid off persons.

Teachers under the age of 45 are generally healthy and able to adapt to the plateau working environment. No limitation is given to the age of teachers in xinjiang. If retired teachers are selected, they must be under the age of 65 and in good health, and they have the will to support education.

4. Policy treatment

(1)The treatment of teachers assisting Tibet and xinjiang shall be subject to the relevant provisions of the "administrative measures on counterpart support for Tibetan cadres and talents" (no. 58, 2011) and "notice on counterpart support for cadres and talents in xinjiang" (no. 6, 2011). During the period of working in xinjiang, the supporting teachers only transferred to organizational relations, not to administrative and wage relations, paid by the original unit, enjoy the same level of the same welfare treatment.

Referring to the relevant provisions of the notice on the treatment of cadres and talents in xinjiang for aid to Tibet (su ren social development [2017] no. 483), Teachers who support education enjoy the following treatment:
①Besides, the monthly living allowance for teachers in Tibet is 3,500 yuan, and the monthly living allowance for teachers in xinjiang is 3,000 yuan.
②Monthly family living allowance of 1,000 yuan for supporting teachers; Every round (one and a half years) supporting teachers to pay 8000 yuan to protect against cold;
④Reimburse the travel expenses for winter and summer vacation once a year, the spouse can enjoy the family leave once a year, the spouse and minor children's travel expenses can be reimbursed once a year.
The above funds from the provincial counterpart support funds listed, by the provincial counterpart support front command coordinated implementation, unified release, reimbursement.

The salary plan for teachers in Tibet shall be implemented in accordance with the "group-style" policy for teachers in Lhasa, and the salary standard (including subsidy) for teachers in xinjiang shall be calculated and issued in accordance with the salary standard (including subsidy) of the same or the same local level.

The sending unit shall, in the name of organization, offer condolences to the family members (spouse, parents and children) of the supporting teachers during the annual festival (Spring Festival, Mid-Autumn festival, etc.), and the necessary expenses shall be listed in the welfare funds of the unit.

The local education bureau shall be responsible for other related work of supporting teachers, including training before going to Tibet and xinjiang. Insurance for personal accident and injury not less than 3 million yuan for supporting teachers; Organize teacher physical examination once a year. The financial requirements are borne by peers.

(2)Volunteer teachers in xinjiang work experience as a rural grassroots teaching experience, under the same conditions of the promotion of professional titles. Those who are qualified for a higher level of professional and technical position in xinjiang during their work in xinjiang may, upon authorization, participate in the assessment of professional and technical position in local organizations in Tibet and xinjiang or in jiangsu province. When participating in jiangsu province appraisal, may exempt the lecture, the defense and so on the link. After the completion of the voluntary teaching period back to the original dispatched units, after examination qualified can be in Tibet, in xinjiang professional and technical posts to be appointed.

(3)When recommending teachers at all levels for honor commendation and merit evaluation, local and local units shall give priority to recommending and selecting teachers for supporting education under the same conditions; In the selection of cadres, under the same conditions, priority should be given to promoting outstanding teachers.

(4)Strengthen the performance appraisal and evaluation of supporting teachers. Under the unified leadership of jiangsu counterpart support front headquarters, it will be jointly organized and implemented by the education department in the place of selection and the place of receiving assistance. In particular, the application of assessment results should be emphasized and correct guidance should be established. Excellent teachers who have excellent performance and are popular at the grassroots level during the supporting education period will be given priority in evaluation and commendation in Tibet and xinjiang. Tibet and xinjiang will give commendations and awards to teachers who have made outstanding contributions. Those who are incompetent or have problems in the assessment during the period of supporting education shall be dealt with in accordance with relevant regulations.

5. Organization and implementation

(1)Local education bureaus, development and reform commissions, finance bureaus, human resources and social security bureaus and other departments should coordinate, guide, coordinate and follow up the work of supervision according to their respective functions, and jointly organize and implement the "Tibet support education program for 10,000 teachers in xinjiang".

(2)Each send a city divided into districts ZhiJiaoTuan established, clear of personnel as a head (involving multiple counties in each recipient sure a deputy chief of counties and cities), responsible for the group teaching in teachers' management, coordination, communication, psychological counseling, etc, in front of the headquarters, ShouYuanDe education bureau, and provincial partnerships sent to bureau of education, especially the station in front of the working group, keep in close contact, timely reflect the problem, we will do our best to help the team service management work.

(3)During the period of supporting and teaching for xinjiang teachers in aid of Tibet, they were jointly managed by the sending unit and the receiving area, and accepted the organizational leadership of the provincial counterpart support headquarters. Province counterpart support in front of the headquarters responsible for teaching in teachers during the hidden in the xinjiang daily management and service work, and realized the front of the teacher group clear ZhiJiaoTuan, sent to, and with ShouYuanDe education administrative department, the school party organization and docking, the implementation of the teaching and life safeguard and the benefits of a teacher's work, coordinate to solve the problem, ensure smooth completion of teaching tasks.

(4)All local schools should mobilize the enthusiasm of teachers to take part in supporting education, take aid to Tibet as an important channel for teachers to train and improve their ability, attach importance to the follow-up development of supporting education teachers, and form a benign mechanism that teachers are willing to support and strive for supporting education.

(5)The first batch of supporting teachers will be in place before the school begins in autumn 2018. Local governments should follow up the plan and implement it. The overall time arrangement is as follows: in May, all regions shall carry out mobilization and recommendation, and report to teachers for supporting education; In June, the provincial department of education audited and confirmed the objects of supporting teachers; In early July, the provincial education department organized a unified connection with Tibet, xinjiang and other places receiving aid. Mid-july organized training in Tibet; Do all the work before the end of July.

Please submit the list of qualified teachers for supporting education (annex 2), the official appointment and removal examination form in triplicate, and the list of persons in charge of the selection work (annex 3) to the provincial education department (electronic version) before June 15. Before the selection, we should communicate with the provincial department of education in advance, and then organize the candidates to the tertiary hospitals according to the requirements of the cadres and talents in Tibet and xinjiang. After the physical examination is completed, the provincial department of education will publish the list of supporting teachers.
