Notice on doing a good job in supporting education in "three districts" in 2018

Education department of hunan province Time: 2018-05-30 00:00

Notice on doing a good job in supporting education in "three districts" in 2018

Education (physical education) bureau of each city, education (physical education) bureau of each relevant county and urban district, relevant provincial secondary and primary schools (kindergartens) :

For the implementation of the state and our province to the requirement of accurate, rural teachers' support for poverty alleviation plan, according to the general office of the ministry of education of about teachers plan 2018 "three areas" talent support special plan announcement concerning the implementation of work (teachers hall letter [2018] no. 4) spirit, do it right now, be a teacher in our province in 2018 "three areas" work notice is as follows:

1. Complete the teaching task of "three districts" in 2018

(1) further strengthen policy publicity and implementation. "Three districts" supporting education work is an extremely important talent support plan of the country, and an important measure to implement the spirit of targeted poverty alleviation, rural teacher support plan, and balanced development spirit of rural and urban obligation education. It provides strong talent support for improving the quality of school teachers in "three districts" and promoting the reform and development of "three districts" education. Secri wing and related administrative departments of education and the school, should further attaches great importance to the "three areas" teaching work, in-depth study and implement, the full implementation of the education department of hunan province and five departments about print and distribute < outlying and poverty-stricken areas and ethnic minority areas and the old revolutionary base areas of hunan province talent support teachers' special plan implementation plan > notification "(xiang teach [2013] no. 559) and other countries and be a teacher in our province" three areas "spirit of the relevant policy documents. According to the current professional title evaluation policy of our province, intermediate positions in urban primary and middle schools should have more than 1 year working experience in rural schools or weak schools, and senior positions should have more than 2 years working experience in rural schools or weak schools. On April 11, 2017, the provincial people's government issued the implementation opinions on accelerating the development of national education (xiang zhengfa [2017] no Teachers who have been teaching in the three districts for one year or more can be identified as having a working experience of more than two years in rural schools or weak schools. This provision provides strong policy support for further teaching in the "three districts", which should be widely publicized and fully utilized by various schools.

(2) further increase the intensity of dispatch. According to the notice of the general office of the ministry of education on the implementation of the teacher special plan of the "three districts" talent support plan for 2018 (department of teachers [2018] no. 4), our province will select 1275 teachers in 2018 (58 more than in 2017), including education 1195 compulsory teachers and 80 non-compulsory education teachers. Education administrative departments and relevant schools in cities, states and relevant counties and cities shall complete the teaching tasks of "three districts" in 2018 with high quality. In principle, sending schools should select backbone teachers with intermediate or higher professional and technical posts, kindergarten teachers can relax the conditions properly, and school administrators should have strong organizational leadership and rich school management experience. The number of non-compulsory education volunteers should account for about 6.27% of the total (rounding). The completion status is included in the annual assessment of municipal and state education administrative departments. Among them, the administrative department of sending prefectural education did not complete the number of dispatched teachers as calculated in xiang jiantong [2013] document no. 559 in 2017 (see the table of dispatched field dispatch ability). In 2018, the number of dispatched teachers must be no less than 1.1 times of the actual number of dispatched teachers in 2017 (rounding). In other cities and states, the principle is to accomplish the task of sending no less than 1.05 times the number of teachers actually sent in 2017 (four rounding off five). Was made about the primary and secondary school (kindergarten) affiliated to consider from long-term development of the teachers and schools, every year there are planning to send teachers teaching, avoid the title appraisal related teachers lack of rural schools and weak school experience, in which each was made in 2018 affiliated middle and primary school (kindergarten) shall generally be sent 1 - three teachers to participate in the "three areas" teaching.

(3) further strengthen communication and coordination. In order to facilitate communication and communication, the "three districts" supporting education administrators such as education (body) bureau of city, state and county should add "work". Please contact my office directly and arrange it uniformly. The administrative departments of education, sending schools, education administrative departments and receiving schools in the province should further strengthen communication and coordination, highlighting the effectiveness of supporting education. Sending out county and city districts should further strengthen the investigation and research, identify the needs of the aided county and district, select the required discipline teachers and key teachers in line with the requirements, and effectively help the aided schools to solve practical problems and improve the teaching level.

2. Do a good job in the teaching management of "three districts"

(1) please, all relevant departments and schools around the in-depth study and implement the education department of hunan province and five departments about print and distribute < outlying and poverty-stricken areas and ethnic minority areas in hunan province and the old revolutionary base areas talents support program teachers special personnel and funds management method >, < outlying and poverty-stricken areas and ethnic minority areas in hunan province and the old revolutionary base areas talents support program teachers special assistance agreement > notice (xiang teach hair [2014] no. 51), such as "three areas," the spirit of teaching files, seriously do a good job in "three" teaching staff management and money management.

(2) give full play to the leading role of supporting teachers. The selected teachers are generally arranged to teach continuously in schools below the county level, with emphasis placed on township schools and village-level schools (teaching centers). Teachers should be arranged to teach the corresponding courses or professional teaching tasks, and the workload should be appropriate. Besides, local teachers should be helped to improve their teaching level and professional ability through various forms such as collective lesson preparation, lectures and open classes. The education department and the school in the county should arrange the living and working conditions of the supporting teachers and do a good job in the daily management assessment.

(3) enrich the form of selection. Teachers and education managers are encouraged to be selected in a "group" format. Full-time teachers mainly undertake the task subject teaching and class management, organizing teaching and researching activities to carry out business training and teaching, give full play to the exemplary role backbone, with local teachers to form teaching team, drive the recipient schools overall improve teaching level and education management ability, is mainly engaged in education management, school management to promote financial AIDS improve education teaching and management level. The aided schools shall organize teachers, administrators and supporting teachers to work together.

(4) standardize the scope and method of supporting education. The special post plan of university students' internship and supporting education and rural compulsory education stage is not included in the selection scope. Short - term grassroots teaching and support, probation period teachers are not included in the scope of selection. Supporting education provides service to the recipient area in the form of full-time work for one year, and encourages the extension of supporting education or staying on the job. Starting from September 2018, the selection work will be carried out according to the academic year.

(5) strengthening fund management. Each place must press the regulation full amount, on time issue "3 area" support teaching work fund. At the end of this year, our department will organize special supervision and report the found problems in the province, and deal with the illegal and disciplinary problems according to the regulations.

6) highlight the actual effect of supporting education in the "three districts". According to the circular of the general office of the ministry of education on the implementation work of the teachers' special plan for the "three districts" talent support plan for 2018, "provinces are not allowed to send teachers to non-poor areas." Starting this year, the "three districts" supporting education should be comprehensively combined with targeted poverty alleviation and education poverty alleviation. If the supported county or district of our province is a non-poor county (see annex 1 for the list of poor counties), please send the unit to do a good job in communication and coordination with the original county or district and the new one, and submit the report of adjusting the county or district to my office before June 20.

(7) the dispatched place shall solve the problem of teacher gap in the dispatched school through various ways, such as internal adjustment, receiving interns, arranging teachers and post training in the assisted place.

In order to summarize the achievements of supporting education in the "three districts", sort out the typical achievements of supporting education teachers, and promote the implementation of supporting education in the "three districts", all regions should do a good job in submitting information related to supporting education in the "three districts" in 2018.
