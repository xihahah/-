Overall social status

Basic education input is still insufficient, and unbalanced, and the benefit is not high

Poor areas have a weak economic foundation, are financially strapped, and are mostly "bread-and-butter". The financial allocation to education is mainly used for head funds, while education funds are difficult to achieve "three growth" and "three proportion". For public funds in education operating expenses, some places are almost zero, and it is common for education expenses to be levied and not returned or misappropriated at will.

The number of schools is insufficient, the running conditions are poor, and the running quality is relatively low

The shortage of funds led to the shortage of schools in poor areas, poor conditions and backward teaching facilities. The serious lag in the construction of learning facilities in poor areas is not only reflected in the old facilities, but also some of the facilities have security risks. At the same time, some teaching supplies, such as experimental instruments, books, etc. are seriously inadequate, and most of the school buildings, books, experimental equipment and other additions and updates cannot get financial support.
